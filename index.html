Free Soloing vs. Free Climbing

This is called free soloing.

  • The climber is climbing high off the ground (not bouldering)
  • The climber is not using a rope and gear for protection if they fall
  • The climber is using their hands/feet only to progress up the rock

This is called free climbing.

  • The climber is climbing high off the ground (not bouldering)
  • The climber is using a rope and gear for protection if they fall
  • The climber is using their hands/feet only to progress up the rock

But What About Aid Climbing?

This is called aid climbing.

  • The climber is climbing high off the ground (not bouldering)
  • The climber is using a rope and gear for protection if they fall
  • The climber is using the gear to progress up the rock
